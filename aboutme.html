About Me

Biography

My name is Stephany Pena. I grew up in Queens, New York. My parents come from Ecuador, making my sister and I Ecuadorian-American. I attended Metropolitan Expeditionary Learning School and graduated from there in 2019. My major is Emerging Media Technology at CUNY's City Tech.

I enjoy learning languages and besides English, I know Spanish, French, and Japanese. My family's tradition and culture influence me to be curious about other world cultures. They have helped to flourish more interests outside of the generic learning material from the public school system.
